Scenes from the 2023 Tomato Festival

For over 15 years, the University of Missouri has hosted its annual Tomato Festival, celebrating everything tomatoes, peppers, flora, and other fresh produce. As a junior reporter in 2023, I covered the fruitful fest for a general assignment shift. Amused by the tradition, I had to go see it for myself.
