Best Neo Soul and R&B Albums

Combining traditional r&b, soul, jazz and hip-hop, neo-soul and r&b is a fast-rising genre of the late 20th and leading into the 21st century. Catch a vibe with these seven albums.

The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill - Lauryn Hill

1998

The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill is the debut and sole solo album of neo-rnb, hip-hop legend Lauryn Hill. Largely regarded as one of the greatest albums of all time, Hill infuses hip-hop, soul, r&b and rap into a timeless classic on love and life.
